UNDERSTANDING THE PROBLEM

What is Smartphone Addiction in Children?

Smartphone addiction in children is a growing concern affecting their mental health, social development, and academic performance. When excessive screen time becomes compulsive, it can lead to anxiety, depression, sleep disorders, and difficulty focusing on real-world activities.
